ASCON IS PREMISED ON THREE CORE MANDATES

Mrs. C. U. Gayya mni, Director-General

The Administrative Staff College of Nigeria (ASCON) was established through Decree No. 39 of 1973 (now ASCON Act. Cap. 6 Vol. LFN 1990) with a tripod like mandate to provide Training, Research and Consultancy services to organizations in all sectors of the Nigerian economy. In keeping with its mandate, ASCON has been assisting organizations to realize their objectives through effective human capital development. ASCON has done this for (50) fifty years, as it celebrates its golden jubilee anniversary and will continue to do so because this is the raison d’etre for the establishment of the College.

“Our vision for another (50) fifty years and beyond is to sustain and continue to improve on our service delivery capabilities to serve the needs of our various clients, through continuous institutional and human capital development with an eye towards being aware of the needs and challenges of our operating environment” - Mrs C. U. Gayya mni.
